About Us

At Explore Kyoto, we are passionate about showcasing the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of one of Japan's most historic cities. Our goal is to provide immersive travel experiences that connect visitors with Kyoto’s timeless traditions, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant local culture.

Our Story

Explore Kyoto was founded in 2010 with the vision to share Kyoto's unique charm and ancient traditions with the world. Over the years, our team of expert guides and cultural enthusiasts has provided memorable experiences to thousands of visitors from around the globe, all while honoring Kyoto's deep-rooted history and values.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide personalized and culturally enriching travel experiences that educate and inspire visitors about Kyoto’s historical and natural beauty. We believe in fostering a deep connection between travelers and the essence of Kyoto’s ancient way of life.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading cultural travel experience provider in Kyoto, offering unmatched expertise in guiding visitors through the city’s treasures while creating lasting memories. We aim to promote sustainable tourism that preserves the city’s cultural heritage for generations to come.
